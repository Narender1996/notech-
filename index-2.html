About us

Novatech Healthcare, Kolkata, is a pharmaceutical manufacturing company established in the year 2012 in the supervision of Mr. Vikash Modi. We engaged in bringing the society a unique range of pharmaceutical products which are quality tested on different parameters to ensure these are maintained as compliance with the WHO- GMP standards. All our medicines are safe and highly effective. As an integrated healthcare solutions provider, we cater products which includes segments like Analgesic & Anti – Inflammatory, Anti-ulcerant, Anti-Biotic, Bronchodilators, Multivitamins and Antioxidants, Ointments and Liquids. Moreover, we strive for optimal products that meet customer expectations. Novatech Healthcare are working as a team work. All our Suppliers are upholding great role for deliver high-quality products time to time as per our willingness. We have established cordial relations with our customers through Quality Products with Ethical Marketing Support and Professional Work Culture. Our all dealers are star of our team who shines and inspires us every single day to promote our Quality Products in market. Their good work is commendable and we proud to work as a co-partner. Novatech Healthcare have so many opportunities for dedicated professionals who are committed for the welfare of mankind and ready to serve the healthcare industry in a proficient manner, since we are looking for enthusiastic and self-starter with an experience and aspiration to change for a bold new career opportunity.
As a young enterprise we have grown rapidly. The company have Quality Products with Ethical Marketing Support and Professional Work Culture. Our core strength will remain the back bone of sustained and profitable growth in dynamic industry.
Novatech Healthcare recognized as a pharmaceutical company through Quality and Competence. To become a young player in the industry by assuring high quality standards and cost effective products at all times. We want to mark our presence in the society by creating a portfolio as cost effective quality medicines available to all. To maintain our social responsibilities of delivering high quality medicines to all segments of society without compromising on our core strength of good ethics and professional work culture. To become acclaimed pharmaceutical company through development and introduction of wide portfolio of pharmaceutical.
